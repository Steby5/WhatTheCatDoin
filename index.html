Delete Your Account - WhatTheCatDoin

If you would like to request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@whatthecatdoin.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, please include your username and the email address associated with your account.
  3. Our support team will process your request and delete your account within 7 business days.

Types of Data Deleted

When you request the deletion of your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your user profile, including your username and email address.
  • All posts and comments you have made.
  • Any images or media you have uploaded.

For more details on our data retention policy, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
