Data Deletion

If you would like to request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the WhatTheCatDoin app on your device.
  2. Go to your profile page and select the "Delete Account" option.
  3. Confirm the deletion by following the prompts.

The following types of data will be deleted:

  • Your profile information
  • Your posts
  • Your comments
  • Your likes

Some data may be retained for a short period to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.
